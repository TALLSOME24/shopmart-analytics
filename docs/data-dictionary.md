## Key Findings — ShopSmart 2024 Sales

**Total Revenue:** ₦[14221350.00]

**Monthly Performance:** December was the strongest month 
at ₦2,743,000, driven by festive season demand for electronics 
and home goods. February recorded the lowest revenue at ₦434,250, 
reflecting a typical post-January spending slowdown.

**Top Category:** Electronics dominated all categories with 
₦11,576,000 in revenue — accounting for the majority of total 
sales. FMCG was the weakest category at ₦385,100.

**Orders by City:** Lagos led all cities with 23 orders, 
followed by Abuja (15) and Port Harcourt (12), suggesting 
Lagos remains ShopSmart's strongest market.

**Average Order Value:** ₦284,427 per transaction.

---

## Recommendation
ShopSmart should prioritise electronics inventory ahead of 
Q4 and investigate why February performance drops — a targeted 
promotion during that period could recover lost revenue.
