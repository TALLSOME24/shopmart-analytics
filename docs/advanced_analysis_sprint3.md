## Key Findings — ShopSmart 2024 Advanced Analysis

**Cumulative Revenue:** ShopSmart reached ₦14,221,350 in 
cumulative revenue by December 2024. The business crossed 
the ₦10M mark in October, with the final quarter driving 
the strongest growth.

**Top Products per Category:**
- Electronics: iPhone 15 Pro — ₦2,200,000
- Home Goods: Sofa Set 3+2 — ₦288,000
- Fashion: Gucci Belt — ₦185,000
- FMCG: Dangote Sugar 50kg — ₦64,000

**Month over Month Growth:** The biggest revenue drop was 
July (-₦1,146,600) while the biggest jump was September 
(+₦1,831,700) driven by iPhone 15 Pro purchases. February 
remains the weakest month consistently.

**Revenue Contribution:** Electronics dominates with 81.4% 
of all revenue. The remaining three categories combined 
account for only 18.6% — indicating heavy dependence on 
one category.

---

## Recommendations
ShopSmart should diversify revenue across categories to 
reduce risk from Electronics dependence. A focused 
strategy to grow Home Goods and Fashion — which show 
premium products like the Sofa Set and Gucci Belt — 
could significantly improve revenue balance.
