## Key Findings — ShopSmart 2024 Customer Behaviour

**Top 5 Customers:** The highest spending customer was 
CUST-333 with ₦2,200,000, followed by CUST-345 with 
₦1,805,000, CUST-321 with ₦1,377,500, CUST-217 with 
₦1,080,000 and CUST-113 with ₦850,000.

**One-Time Customers:** Every single customer in 2024 made 
only one purchase. ShopSmart currently has zero repeat 
customers — this is a critical issue that needs immediate 
attention from the business.

**Above Average Spenders:** 11 customers spent above the 
average order value of ₦284,427, all concentrated in 
high-value Electronics purchases.

**Top Category Per City:**
- Lagos: Electronics was most purchased (8 orders)
- Abuja: Home Goods was most purchased (5 orders)
- Port Harcourt: Electronics was most purchased (3 orders)

---

## Recommendation
ShopSmart must urgently launch a customer retention and 
loyalty programme. With 100% of customers being one-time 
buyers, the business is entirely dependent on acquiring 
new customers to grow — which is far more expensive than 
retaining existing ones. A targeted email campaign to 
previous customers with Electronics promotions could 
significantly improve repeat purchase rates.
